# ⚡ Kuryentahin: 3-Minute Demo Script

**Theme:** Empowering Filipinos during the Energy Crisis.
**Target Audience:** Panels, Users, or Stakeholders.

---

## 🕒 0:00 - 0:30 | The Hook & The Problem
**Visual:** Show the Splash Screen and the Login Page (Google Sign-In).

> "Kamusta! We are Team Nexus, and we’re here to talk about a problem every Filipino family knows too well: the energy crisis. Whether it’s an unexpected brownout in the middle of a workday or the rising cost of electricity, we’ve always been in the dark—literally.
>
> Introducing **Kuryentahin**. Think of it as 'Waze for the Philippine Energy Crisis.' It’s a community-powered platform designed to make sure no Filipino is left without information or help when the lights go out."

---

## 🕒 0:30 - 1:15 | Feature 1: The Live Brownout Map
**Visual:** Navigate to the **Brownout Map**. Tap a marker.

> "Our core feature is the **Live Brownout Map**. Here, users can report power interruptions in real-time. But it’s not just about reports—it’s about **verification**. 
>
> Just like Waze, other 'Bayanis' in the area can upvote or downvote reports to confirm if the power is still out. We also integrate **Meralco’s scheduled maintenance** alerts directly onto the map, so you can plan your day *before* the interruption happens. It’s crowdsourced intelligence for every barangay."

---

## 🕒 1:15 - 2:00 | Feature 2: AI Energy Audit (The "Wow" Factor)
**Visual:** Navigate to **Energy Audit**. Tap the **Camera Icon**. Point at a laptop/fan.

> "Now, how do we handle the *cost* of energy? We’ve integrated **Google Gemini AI** to create the smartest Energy Audit tool in the Philippines. 
>
> Simply take a photo of any appliance—like this laptop or electric fan—and our AI instantly identifies it, estimates its wattage, and adds it to your daily calculator. 
>
> It doesn't stop there. The AI then generates **'Makatipid Tips'** in Taglish, giving you personalized advice on how to lower *your* specific monthly bill based on your usage. It’s like having an energy consultant in your pocket."

---

## 🕒 2:00 - 2:45 | Feature 3: Fuel Tracker & Bayanihan
**Visual:** Quick switch to **Fuel Pool** and then the **Bayanihan Board**.

> "During long outages, fuel becomes a lifeline. Our **Fuel Tracker** shows real-time station statuses and community-verified prices. We require at least 3 reports to validate a price, ensuring you never waste a trip to an empty station.
>
> And when things get tough, we turn to the **Bayanihan Board**. This is where the community shines. Whether it’s sharing a generator, offering a charging station, or finding a local business that’s still open during a blackout, we reward our users with **Bayani Points**, turning community service into a point-based ranking system."

---

## 🕒 2:45 - 3:00 | The Conclusion
**Visual:** Show the **Profile Rank** (e.g., Gold Bayani) and end on the Logo.

> "Kuryentahin isn't just an app; it’s a digital movement. We’re moving Filipinos from being victims of the energy crisis to being informed, connected, and empowered Bayanis. 
>
> **Kuryentahin: Para sa Bayan, Para sa Bayanihan.** Maraming salamat!"

---

### 💡 Pro-Tips for the Demo:
*   **Show, Don't Just Tell**: Make sure you are actually tapping the buttons as you speak.
*   **The AI Scan**: This is your strongest technical feature. Spend the most energy here!
*   **Speed**: Keep it snappy. If a screen takes a second to load, keep talking about the *benefit* of that feature.

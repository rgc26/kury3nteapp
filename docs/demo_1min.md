# ⚡ Kuryentahin: 1-Minute LIVE DEMO Script

**Focus:** Fast visual walkthrough of the actual app interface.

---

## 🕒 0:00 - 0:10 | Start & Map
**Action:** Open app to **Live Map**.
> "Kuryentahin starts here: the **Live Brownout Map**. Users can report outages instantly with GPS. See these markers? They are community-verified reports, showing exactly where the power is out right now across the city."

---

## 🕒 0:10 - 0:40 | The AI Vision Audit (Main Highlight)
**Action:** Switch to **Energy Audit** tab → Tap **Camera Icon** → Point at laptop/fan.
> "The heart of our app is the **AI Energy Audit**. Watch this: I take a photo of this appliance. Gemini AI identifies it as a 65W Laptop, estimates the monthly cost, and gives me 'Makatipid Tips' in Taglish on how to save. It’s instant, vision-based energy auditing."

---

## 🕒 0:40 - 0:55 | Community & Bayanihan
**Action:** Switch to **Bayanihan Board** → Tap **Generator** category.
> "When the lights go out, we turn to the **Bayanihan Board**. Here, neighbors share generators and charging stations. Every report and act of help earns you **Bayani Points**, increasing your rank in our community trust system."

---

## 🕒 0:55 - 1:00 | Conclusion
**Action:** Show **Profile Page** with Rank.
> "From data to community action—that’s Kuryentahin. Para sa Bayanihan. Maraming salamat!"

---

### 💡 Speed Tips:
*   **Transition Fast**: Don't wait for animations to finish. Tap the next tab as soon as you've mentioned the current one.
*   **Pre-load**: Have your camera ready and an appliance nearby before starting the timer!
